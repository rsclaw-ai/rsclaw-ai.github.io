# rsclaw channels

Manage messaging channels.

## Subcommands

### `rsclaw channels list`

List configured channels and their status.

### `rsclaw channels login <channel>`

Login to a channel (e.g., WeChat QR scan).

```bash
rsclaw channels login wechat
```

### `rsclaw channels logout <channel>`

Logout from a channel.

### `rsclaw channels test <channel>`

Test channel connectivity.
